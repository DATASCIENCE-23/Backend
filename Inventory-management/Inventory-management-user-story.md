# Inventory Management Module – Overview & Features

## Purpose
Keep track of all non-medicine hospital supplies (gloves, syringes, bandages, cotton, IV sets, surgical tools, test kits, linens, etc.) so the hospital never runs out and doesn’t waste items.

## Main Users
- Store keeper / inventory staff  
- Ward nurses / department staff  
- Procurement / purchase staff  
- Hospital managers / admins  

## Key Features (simple list)

### Item List (Master Data)
- Add, edit, search items  
- Store details: name, code, category, unit, price, expiry tracking, minimum stock level  

### Stock View
- See current quantity in main store and in each department  
- Quick search by name or code  

### Receive New Stock
- Record items arriving from suppliers  
- Support barcode scanning for fast entry  
- Update stock quantity and location  

### Issue Supplies
- Departments request items  
- Approve and give out supplies  
- Stock reduces automatically after issue  

### Low Stock & Expiry Alerts
- Warn when stock goes below minimum level  
- Warn when items are near expiry (e.g., 30/60 days left)  

### Stock Transfer
- Move items from main store to a ward or between departments  

### Stock Adjustment
- Fix count if items are damaged, lost, or counted wrong  
- Record reason for change  

### Stock Audit / Physical Count
- Plan and record periodic stock checks  
- Show differences between system and actual count  

### Reports & Dashboard
- Current stock status  
- Low stock list  
- Near-expiry items  
- Items used most / least  
- Total value of stock  
- Usage by department  

## Simple Benefits for Your Hospital
- Avoid shortages → better patient care  
- Reduce waste from expired items → save money  
- Know exactly what is in stock → less confusion  
- Faster ordering → less panic buying  
- Clear records → easier audits  

## Quick Summary Sentence
The Inventory Module is the hospital’s supply tracker: it shows what you have, warns when you’re running low or items are expiring, helps you receive and give out supplies, and gives clear reports so nothing gets wasted.
