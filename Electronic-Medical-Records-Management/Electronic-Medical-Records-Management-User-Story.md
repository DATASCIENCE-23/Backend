## Overview
The **Electronic Medical Records (EMR)** module is a core component of a Hospital Management System that digitally stores and manages patient clinical information.  
It enables healthcare professionals to access **accurate, real-time, and complete medical data**, improving patient care, safety, and operational efficiency.  
The EMR module integrates with other systems such as **Laboratory (LIS)**, **Radiology (RIS)**, **Pharmacy**, and **Billing**.

## Summary
The EMR module serves as a **digital patient chart**, enabling doctors, nurses, and administrators to efficiently manage and access clinical information.  
By supporting real-time data access, secure record handling, and seamless integration with other hospital systems, the EMR module plays a critical role in improving **patient safety, treatment accuracy, and overall healthcare quality**.
