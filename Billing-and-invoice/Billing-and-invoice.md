# Billing & Invoicing Module – Overview & Features

## Purpose
To manage the financial lifecycle of patient care by accurately recording charges for consultations, procedures, medications, and room stays. It ensures that the hospital captures all revenue, handles insurance claims correctly, and provides transparent billing to patients.

---

## Main Users
- **Billing Clerks / Cashiers** – Generate invoices and collect payments.  
- **Finance & Accounts Team** – Track revenue, outstanding debts, and ensure tax compliance.  
- **Insurance Coordinators** – Manage pre-approvals and third-party settlements.  
- **Hospital Administrators** – Monitor the overall financial health of the facility.

---

## Key Features

### Service & Tariff Management
- Maintain a master list of services (Consultations, Lab tests, Radiology, Surgery).  
- Flexible pricing based on patient category (General, VIP, Corporate, Insurance).  
- Automatic updates for taxes and service charges.

### Real-Time Billing (OPD & IPD)
- **Outpatient (OPD):** Instant billing for walk-in consultations and quick tests.  
- **Inpatient (IPD):** Continuous billing that accumulates charges for room rent, nursing, and medicines during a patient's stay.

### Insurance & TPA Integration
- Track insurance eligibility and co-payment amounts.  
- Separate billing for *Patient Payable* and *Company Payable* portions.  
- Manage credit limits for corporate clients.

### Payment & Receipting
- Support multiple payment modes: Cash, Credit/Debit Cards, Digital Wallets, and Bank Transfers.  
- Handle advance deposits and partial payments.  
- Automatically generate professional, branded receipts.

### Discounts & Adjustments
- Apply authorized discounts (Staff, Senior Citizen, Government schemes).  
- Process refunds for cancelled services or overpayments.  
- Issue credit notes for billing corrections.

### Financial Reports & Analytics
- Daily cash collection summaries.  
- Revenue by department (e.g., Lab vs. Pharmacy).  
- Aging reports for outstanding invoices.  
- Tax reports (VAT / GST / Service Tax).

---

## Simple Benefits for Your Hospital
- **Zero Leakage:** Ensures every syringe used and every test done is billed.  
- **Faster Discharge:** Automated billing reduces patient waiting time.  
- **Professionalism:** Detailed, error-free invoices build trust with patients and insurers.  
- **Financial Clarity:** Real-time visibility into income and outstanding dues.

---

## Quick Summary
**The Billing Module is the financial heart of the hospital; it converts clinical actions into professional invoices, manages insurance complexities, and tracks every cent from the moment a patient arrives until their final receipt is issued.**
